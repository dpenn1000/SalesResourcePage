How it works

Three steps. Nothing to install on your end.

1
A member names your organization.
When a member of your community asks Trinity Solar for a quote, they tell us who sent them. That is the whole referral.
2
Trinity does the work.
Custom proposal, design, permits, install, paperwork, warranty. Your member saves on their electric bill. You stay out of it.
3
Your organization gets paid.
A check arrives when the appointment happens, and again when the install closes. The top partners on the program have collected $80,000 or more.

Why partners stay

The program asks for nothing the organization has to spend.

✓
No cost to join.No setup fee, no contract, no minimum. If no one refers, no one is out anything.
✓
No fundraising event to run.The donations come from regular member purchases, not from a gala or a 5K.
✓
Payments arrive after each install.You see money throughout the year, not in one lump at the end of a campaign.
✓
Trinity is the one being judged.Your members work with a 30-year-old regional installer. We carry the warranty.
✓
It compounds.Partners who hit five-figure totals all started with one member, one quote.
✓
Members win too.The same homeowner who funded your check is the one saving on their electric bill for the next 25 years.
