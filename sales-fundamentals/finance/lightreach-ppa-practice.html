PPA Section 1Introduction

This Power Purchase Agreement is entered into by and between you and Palmetto Solar, LLC, DBA LightReach, as of the Effective Date for the sale and purchase of all electric energy generated by the System to be installed on or at your Home. LightReach agrees to sell to you, and you agree to buy from LightReach, all of the power produced by the System on the terms and conditions described in this Power Purchase Agreement.

You may manage your obligations and communications via LightReach's online customer portal at palmetto.com/products/palmetto-app, or email help@palmetto.com.

PPA Section 2PPA Term

LightReach agrees to sell you power generated by the System for a minimum of twenty-five (25) years following your Interconnection Date. The PPA Term begins on the "Interconnection Date," which is the date that the System has been approved by your utility to be connected to the utility grid and begin generating power.

Approximate Start Date: installation commences within thirty (30) business days from the date that the later of all permits are issued, any required HOA approval letter is received, or all materials are delivered.

Approximate Interconnection Date: all work to install the System shall be completed as soon as possible after but in no event more than three (3) months from the Commencement Date.

PPA Section 4PPA Payments, Amounts, Purchase, Prepayment & Renewal
PPA Section 4
Payments
Say it like this

"$0 deposit, it comes out automatically each month, and here are a couple of practical things."

If they stop
  • Set up autopay, or it's $15 more a month.
  • Tax credits belong to the owner. Standard for a product you don't own.
  • Buyout at fair market value from year 5. Prepay at a 5% discount.
Autopay is the one to land proactively.

a. No Deposit. There is no deposit for this Agreement.

b. Pricing. During the first year of the term, the purchase price is $0.180 per kWh. After the first year, the price per kWh will increase in accordance with Exhibit F.

Your Monthly Payment will be the product of the annual price per kWh multiplied by the estimated kWh output for the calendar year ("Estimated Annual Production") divided by twelve (12) months, plus any applicable taxes.

c. Taxes. Any applicable sales, transaction, or property taxes are your responsibility. All Tax Credits, incentives, RECs, capacity rights, value of solar tariffs, net metering benefits, and any other applicable benefits are exclusively the property of LightReach and its partners.

d. Payment Method; Late Payment. The Monthly Payment amount assumes automatic payments. If you pay by any other method, your Monthly Payment will be increased by fifteen dollars ($15.00). A late payment fee of $25 will be assessed after the twentieth (20th) day past due.

e. Purchase Request. If you are not in default, you may request to purchase the System at the fifth (5th) anniversary of the Interconnection Date, any time after, or if you sell the Home. The purchase price will be the fair market value of the System.

f. Prepayment. At any time, you may prepay all expected Monthly Payments at a five percent (5%) discount, as set forth in Exhibit F.

g. Renewal. The Agreement will be automatically renewed for an additional one (1) year term at the end of the Initial Term, with 60 days' notice and options to renew, allow auto-renewal, or not renew.

LightReach will install the System per plans, provide a production meter, insure the System against damage (with exceptions), require Certified Installers to maintain workers compensation, employers liability, commercial general liability ($1M per occurrence, $2M annual aggregate), and commercial automobile liability ($1M), and not place a lien on your Home or Property.

PPA Section 10Selling Your Home

If you sell the Home, you may: (i) transfer the PPA to the buyer subject to credit approval and execution of a transfer assignment; (ii) purchase the System at fair market value and sell the Home with the System owned; or (iii) prepay the remaining Monthly Payments at the discount set forth in Exhibit F.

PPA Section 11Loss or Damage

LightReach bears the risk of loss or damage to the System, except where caused by your gross negligence, your intentional damage, or your failure to adhere to your System, Home, and Property Maintenance obligations under Section 5.

PPA Section 12Limitation of Liability

IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE TO THE OTHER FOR INCIDENTAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, INDIRECT, SPECIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES ARISING OUT OF OR RELATING TO THIS AGREEMENT. EACH PARTY'S TOTAL LIABILITY SHALL NOT EXCEED THE AMOUNTS PAID OR PAYABLE UNDER THIS AGREEMENT IN THE TWELVE MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM.

PPA Sections 13 to 14Default and Remedies

Either party may default by failing to perform a material obligation under this Agreement. Default may include failure to pay, refusal of access, removal of the System without authorization, or breach of representations. Remedies include cure rights, acceleration of remaining Monthly Payments at the discounted prepayment amount, removal of the System, and reasonable enforcement costs.

PPA Section 15Returning the System at the End or Upon Termination

Upon expiration or termination, you may purchase the System at fair market value, renew the Agreement under Section 4(g), or allow LightReach to remove the System at LightReach's expense, restoring your roof to a watertight condition.

PPA Sections 16 to 25Additional Terms

Includes Additional Agreements, Applicable Law and Arbitration, Waiver and Severability, Notices, Entire Agreement, Headings and Interpretation, Publicity, Communications Equipment, Counterparts, and Further Assurances. Standard provisions found in commercial home-improvement and energy agreements.
