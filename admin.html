Powers the rep-facing Grid Edge Checker. Refresh once a year when CT ESS publishes the new Grid Edge Criteria Circuit Map. Paste the new map's zip + circuit list below; the checker uses the most recent version automatically.

Commission Settings

The single source of truth for the Commission Calculator: pay rates, lead fees, minimums, club adders, and club qualifiers. Change a value here and the calculator picks it up on its next load. No code edits.

Percent values are entered as percents (4% not 0.04). Base PPW and the rep-control cap are plain numbers. Edits are restricted to admins and VPs.
