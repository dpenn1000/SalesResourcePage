The Top 10 things that look broken but aren't

Most "errors" in oneBUTTON aren't bugs. They're a missing step, a wrong spot, or a sync that hasn't caught up yet. Here are the ten that come up most, what each one actually means, and the fix that works. Ranked by how often reps hit them.

Read the signal first

Every entry below carries a signal light. It tells you, before you read another word, whether this is a thirty-second self-fix, something to verify, a stop-and-protect-the-deal moment, or a vendor problem that was never yours. Match the message on your screen to the entry, then run the fix.

When you can name what oneBUTTON is actually doing, you stop fighting it. Most of these you fix yourself, faster than it takes to write the support ticket.

// the ranked ten

What it means, and the fix that works

No. 01

Credit passed in LightReach, but oneBUTTON won't let you sign

Self-fix
on screenCredit needs to be ran · agreement section blank · submit-quote button gone

LightReach doesn't always push the updated credit status back to oneBUTTON. The credit is fine. OB just hasn't heard about it. This is the single most-reported issue in the whole channel.

The fix
  • Click Finance Partner Sync to re-pull the credit. It comes in within 15 to 20 seconds and the agreement opens.
  • Don't route through the LightReach portal, and don't rebuild the deal.
Still stuck after a re-sync? BT chat + DL#
No. 02

"Pitch out of tolerance," 0 panels, or the design won't load

Verify
on screenOne or more arrays have a pitch that is not between 5° and 45°

The design never made it from oneDRAW into oneBUTTON, so OB is reading an empty design (0 panels, bad pitch). The pitch usually isn't the real problem.

The fix
  • Refinalize the design in oneDRAW, refresh the oneBUTTON design page, hit Save.
  • If OB says the design is locked (Pre-Install Certified), you can't save it yourself. Chatter the RSA on the Salesforce opportunity.
  • On the pitch number: the system enforces 45°. LightReach has agreed to allow 50° but hasn't been updated yet. If a real pitch between 45° and 50° is blocking you, flag your manager.
Design problems route to: RSA via SF chatter
No. 03

"Some error occurred in create lead request" / "Sales person not assigned"

Self-fix
on screenSome error occured in Create lead request

Almost always the same cause: the Trinity Salesperson field on the Salesforce opportunity is empty, or your assignment hasn't synced yet. Being the opportunity owner isn't enough.

The fix
  • Assign yourself as Trinity Salesperson in Salesforce, wait for the sync, retry.
  • If it's set and still failing: clear the field, save, re-enter your name, save again, then refresh oneBUTTON.
Still failing: BT chat + DL#
No. 04

The combopack shows the wrong design (smaller than you sold)

Stop
on screenCombopack renders a smaller system than the one you sold

This one is different from the rest of the list. It is not a glitch you can regenerate away, and it is the one that can actually cost you. A wrong design in the combopack almost always means the finance contract underneath is wrong too.

The fix
  • Check the signed finance contract's system size in Salesforce against what you sold. There is no trustworthy quick fix here.
Read the full breakdown: The drift trap →
No. 05

The combopack never showed up in Box

Verify
on screenNo combopack, no audit-trail email, Box folder empty after signing

Four things cause this, most common first: a signer hasn't actually finished (often a secondary contact); the Box folders weren't created; a new quote voided the old contract and restarted signing; or the opportunity was open in two tabs.

The fix
  • Check for a pending signer first. The combopack only lands in Box after every signer completes.
  • Don't close your laptop until the final "complete" confirmation. One tab, one editor per opportunity.
  • If folders are missing, re-finalize the design to regenerate them.
Everyone signed and it's still missing: BT chat + DL#
No. 06

"Customer is not in one button" / can't find it / 2nd meter missing

Self-fix
on screenCustomer is not in one button

You just created the opportunity (or a second meter) and it's not there yet. Nothing's broken. Salesforce-to-oneBUTTON sync runs on a delay.

The fix
  • Wait. New opportunities take about 45 to 90 minutes, an assignment change about 10 to 15.
  • Open it using the oneBUTTON link from Salesforce, not by searching the name, and confirm you're the Trinity Salesperson.
Over 90 minutes: BT chat + DL#
No. 07

Everything's spinning and you can't quote (and neither can your teammates)

Not you
on screenWe are experiencing loading delays with the portal...

The portal keeps spinning, quotes won't generate. The tell: other reps are reporting the exact same thing in the same window. That means LightReach or oneBUTTON is down, not your deal. On one 55-minute outage, six reps panicked independently.

The fix
  • Wait a few minutes and retry.
  • Don't rebuild the deal, and don't re-report. If a teammate already flagged it, BT knows.
Tell: several reps, same issue, same window.
No. 08

Can't change the primary, remove a signer, or fix an email after credit

Verify
on screenCannot change primary contact · unable to remove second signer

Once credit is in flight, identity locks. You can't pull off an accidental second signer, correct the email, or swap a title name for a legal name.

The fix
  • Prevention beats the cure: before you run credit, confirm the exact legal name on title and the correct email.
  • After the fact: a dev reset and a new lead (post in BT chat with the DL# and who should be primary), or for a name-on-title mismatch, LightReach makes the change on their side.
Reset path: BT chat + DL#
No. 09

Second meter at the same address won't sync

Self-fix
on screenfailed to sync... a contract has already been created

Two meters, same street address, and the second one fails because OB thinks a contract already exists. The second meter is reusing the first meter's finance lead. Only one contract can live on one lead.

The fix
  • On Finance Partner Sync, uncheck "Select Lead" and click Create New Finance Partner Lead so the second system gets its own.
If contacts are locked/duplicated: BT chat + DL#
No. 10

"Slope data for this site is not available" / roof squares won't pull

Self-fix
on screenSlope data for this site is not available, Please enter override value to proceed.

A roof-square or slope error blocks the quote. EagleView didn't return roof data for the address.

The fix
  • Run the roof-square refresh (the "Refreshing Roof Squares in OB/OD" Scribe guide), or enter a known Normal/Low override.
  • When you override, leave a field blank if you don't know it. Don't type 0. A 0 reads as a real value and breaks the quote.
Persisting roof data gaps: RSA via SF chatter
// no. 04, in full

The drift trap

Of the ten, this is the only one that can quietly cost you a deal, so it gets its own breakdown. The combopack pulling the wrong design is the symptom. The cause sits upstream, in the finance contract.

CONTRACT INTEGRITY

When the combopack shows the wrong size, the contract underneath is usually wrong too

When you push a design and run credit, the finance partner locks that system. If the design changes after that and doesn't get pushed back to the finance partner, oneBUTTON shows the new system while the finance partner still holds the old one. The quote, the contract, and the combopack all serve the old, locked design. The combopack is just where you finally see it.

So regenerating the combopack does nothing, because it re-pulls the same wrong contract. And re-saving the design does not fix a finance contract that is already locked.

✓ Signed contract is correct

The combopack is just stale paper. Post in BT chat to refresh the design intent on the combopack. Nothing else owed.

✗ Signed contract is wrong

The customer needs to be re-signed on the right system. Void the wrong contract, build a corrected quote, and re-sign. Post in BT chat with the DL# so dev can clear the way.

"It looks right now" is not the same as "the signed contract is right." We have seen deals where the rep thought it was handled and the customer was never actually signed on the system they bought. Always verify the signed contract.

// false alarms

These scare people and mean nothing

"No Contracts have been generated for this opportunity."
You haven't advanced a quote to contract yet. Do that and it fills in.
"Below the Floor" / "Pricing Below the Floor"
Informational only. If your price is at or above the floor, proceed.
Roof cost rounding looks "off"
oneBUTTON mirrors the roofing calculator. Same inputs, same totals. Expected.
"No button for title search"
Title is already populated and synced. Nothing to do.
