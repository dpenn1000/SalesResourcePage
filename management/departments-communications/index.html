@ContractWriting

Sales Support

The front door of Sales Operations
Phone & Email
⚠️
Email is for everyone. Any rep can email Sales.Ops@Trinity-Solar.com. The phone line is only for Direct reps with an in-home revision.
💡
The most common mix-up. Reps open a new email for a Track C issue. Track C lives on one thread: Reply All to the existing Track C email instead of starting a new one. Document Processing owns those.
What they do

Triage the shared mailbox and the phone lines, then route each request to the right team. They back up Direct reps in-home to close the deal. The team is cross-trained from LOI submission through post-install.

When to reach out

Any request, a change order, a post-signing system change, a question, goes here by email. Direct reps in-home needing a revision now: call. For a Track C item, Reply All to the existing Track C thread instead of starting a new email.

Document Processing

Also called Project Tracking
@ProjecTracking
What they do

Review each signed deal at Stage 4. They confirm the photos, design, feasibility, utility and equipment details, and usage data are all there, then assign a track: A clean, B minor follow-up, C held with an email to the rep.

When to reach out

After signing, when a document or tracking issue is holding a project back. They own the Track C emails: for a Track C item, Reply All to the existing thread instead of starting a new one.

Pre-Install Change Orders

Pre-install only
@SO: Pre-Install ChangeOrders
What they do

Work the Sales Approval queue and every pre-install change order. They follow up on held items, chase customer signatures, and handle account creation and intervention. Post-install change orders belong to a separate team under Finance Partner Operations.

When to reach out

You need a post-signing change to the system. Reps can't run their own change order, so email Sales.Ops to request one and this team works it. If the deal is still on Track C, reply to the Track C thread instead.

Finance Partner Operations

In progress

A separate organization under Kristi Turner, who reports to Kurt Wingenfeld, the same VP the Sales Ops directors report to. It was pulled out of Sales Operations in summer 2025 and made its own department, and now runs parallel to it. The team owns all milestone submissions for every finance partner, post–Stage 5 contract review and processing, and all post-install change orders. Hours are 8am–5pm Monday–Friday; overnight staff handle data entry and milestone processing but do not interface with reps. Confirmed by Kristi Turner, June 9, 2026.

📧
How to reach the team. Email milestones@trinity-solar.com or changeorders@trinity-solar.com depending on the topic — and reserve these inboxes for escalated customers, not general questions. They are a small team with limited bandwidth; the inboxes are triaged for the most urgent situations, so “general” or “what’s the latest” emails are deprioritized. Route general finance-partner questions through your manager so they can be handled at a higher level. Do not use Chatter to reach this team.
