How leads get loaded

Leads sync automatically every time the Appointment Parser runs on the local dashboard (port 8765). The parser reads all snapshot files, classifies opportunities as No Show, Cancelled, Go Back, or No Sale, and upserts them into Supabase. Once a lead is in Supabase, you can assign it, update its status, and log contacts here.

To add new leads: run the parser from the local Appointment Dashboard, or use the manual form below for one-off entries.
