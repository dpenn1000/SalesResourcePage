Is this address on the Grid Edge?

Grid Edge homes qualify for a $130/kWh enrollment incentive instead of $30/kWh, a 4× difference. Confirm it before you walk in. Start with the quick visual check, then confirm the exact circuit for anything borderline.

1 Quick check
Is the home next to a colored line?
Search the address in the Eversource map. A home sitting on or right beside a blue or red line is very likely Grid Edge.
Open Eversource map in new tab → United Illuminating map
How to read it
  1. Search the customer's address.
  2. A blue or red line on or next to the parcel = likely Grid Edge. (UI: red lines.)
  3. Borderline, or two homes close together? Confirm the exact circuit in Step 2, since adjacent parcels can be on different circuits.
2 Confirm the circuit
Look up the parcel's circuit
For a definitive answer, enter the address in Eversource's Hosting Capacity Map, click the parcel, and read the circuit number from the popup.
Open full map in new tab → United Illuminating map
How to read it
  1. Search the customer's address in the map.
  2. Click directly on the home's parcel.
  3. In the popup, find the Circuit field (e.g. 5R7 or 412M1).
  4. Type the zip and that circuit in Step 3 below.
3 Check Grid Edge status
Zip code + circuit number
Type the zip first. We'll show the Grid Edge circuits in that zip, then enter the parcel's circuit.
