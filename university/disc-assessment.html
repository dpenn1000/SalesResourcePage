Before you start

There are no right or wrong answers, and this is not a test you pass or fail. It measures how you naturally sell, not how you think a good rep should sell. Answer honestly and fast. Your gut answer is the accurate one.

How it works

Two parts, about 8 to 12 minutes total.

Part 1 (24 questions). Each one gives you four ways to react in the field. Pick the one most like you and the one least like you.

Part 2 (12 questions). Rate how much you agree with each statement, 1 to 5.

Why bother? Your natural style works great on buyers who think like you, and quietly loses the other three quarters of the market. Once you can see your default, you can switch out of it on purpose. That is the whole game. This pairs with the Know Your Buyer training.
